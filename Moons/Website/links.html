For a great description of Jupiter and its moons, go to Bill Arnett's The Nine Planets at:
http://seds.lpl.arizona.edu/billa/tnp/nineplanets.html

Spectacular images of the Jovian system from NASA's Galileo space probe can be found at:
http://galileo.jpl.nasa.gov

Mike Guidry at the University of Tennessee has written a nice applet demonstrating Kepler's laws.
