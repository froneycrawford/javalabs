Welcome to Voyages JavaLab!

This applet is part of an interactive java series which explores dynamical astrophysics. In this applet, we explore spacecraft trajectories and energy requirements for interplanetary travel. Using the applets contained in here, you can send a spacecraft to Mars, or to tour the outer planets in what is known as a "Grand Tour."
The menu to the left will lead you to the following information:
  • BACKGROUND: Scientific background information behind orbits and transfer orbits
  • APPLET: Launches the applets
  • CONTROLS: Information about running the applets
  • APPLET PHYSICS: The physics behind the applets
  • LAB: Exercises for the user to explore spacecraft orbits
  • LINKS: External links for additional information
